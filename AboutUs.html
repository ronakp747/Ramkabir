1649 to 1737

Jivanji Maharaj’s Life span as per Samagam compiled by Haridas Nadiyada

1649

Birth of Jivanji Maharaj in Karamsad Village near Anand, Gujarat

1660

Reached Gahej Goriyad on Shravan Sud 8 after leaving home in desire of spiritual quest.

1665

Met Guru Gopaldas ji in village Kesulu (Kesumbal) first time

1660 to 1683

Wandered for 23 years here & there in quest of true spirituality.

1683

Meet Guru Gopaldas ji in Choranda near Karjan Gujarat on Aso Sud 13 and had discourse 2N1D.

1683

Endorse and Recognize Gopaldas ji as Guru and Embrace RAMKABIR tradition on Sharad Purnima.

1687

Went on to Pilgrimage with Guru Gopaldas ji to Malwa and North India on Aso Sud 10

1687

Guru Gopaldas ji Swadham Gaman after few days after went on to Pilgrimage.

1687

Guru Gopaldas ji Swadham Gaman after few days after went on to Pilgrimage.

1687 to 1688

During Pilgrimage some important events took place:

- Reach to village Dantnagar near Badrinath in Himalaya, and from there he sighted Gujarat as his place to settled.

- Met Narandas Khatri in Bareli UP, who then became first disciple of Jivanji Maharaj.

- Met Nana Gopaldas in Chanderia MP, who then became second disciple of Jivanji Maharaj later followed by 9 other family.

- Resorted in village Vadsar after entering Gujarat.

- First Ochhav: in village Samiala Dist Vadodara.

- Second Ochhav: in Dhaniyavi Dist Vadodara at Haridas Nadiada’s House.

1688

Manifest Uda Parampara (Tradition) and Emergence of Uda Bhakta Samaj on Sharad Purnima at village Shahpura Dist Vadodara Gujarat.

1709

Lush appearance of flourishing vegetation of Vaal Papadi farm during hot summer days on Vaishakh Sud 5 at village Shahpura

1709

Sant Samagam on Dada Sukhji (Dada Guru) on Bhadarva Vad 13

1710

Sant Samagam on Jagat (Maya) no Brahma on Chaitra Sud 10

1716

Compassion to Dhanbai at village Mangleshwar on Maghasar Sud 2

1716

Went on second Pilgrimage on Jestha Sud 2

1726

Went on third Pilgrimage to Dwarka on Jestha Sud 2

1734

Liberate Women from menstruation curse by passing ordinance on Asadha Sud 2 (Rath Yaatra)

1737

First Term assignation for last voyage on Maghasar Sud 2 at Bhilapur

1737

Second Term assignation for last voyage on Jestha Sud 2 at Puniyad

1737

Last Samagam on Shravan Vad 8 at Puniyad

1737

Final Voyage Swadhan Gaman on Shravan Vad 8 at Puniyad

1737

Anand Ochhav, Matru Shri Harmabaa Swadham Gaman on Bhadarva Sud 5
